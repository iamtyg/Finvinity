üì± Platform-Specific Policies

Apple App Store

Even though Finvinity is free, if you ever encounter issues with any potential future purchases or if Apple mistakenly charges you:

How to Request a Refund from Apple

  1. Via Apple's Website:
    • Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
    • Sign in with your Apple ID
    • Find the purchase and click "Report a Problem"
    • Select your reason and submit the request
  2. Via Settings App:
    • Go to Settings > [Your Name] > iTunes & App Store
    • Tap your Apple ID and select "View Apple ID"
    • Tap "Purchase History" and find the item
    • Tap "Report a Problem"
  3. Contact Apple Support:
    • Visit getsupport.apple.com
    • Select "Billing & Subscriptions"
    • Choose to chat, call, or email Apple

Apple's Refund Policy

  • Timeframe: Requests must be made within 90 days of purchase
  • Approval: Apple reviews each request individually
  • Processing Time: Refunds typically process within 48 hours to 30 days
  • Method: Refunds return to your original payment method

Google Play Store

For Android users, Google Play also has refund policies for any potential future purchases:

How to Request a Refund from Google Play

  1. Via Google Play Console:
    • Go to play.google.com/store/account
    • Find the purchase under "Order history"
    • Select "Request a refund" or "Report a problem"
    • Follow the on-screen instructions
  2. Via Google Play App:
    • Open Google Play Store app
    • Tap Menu > Account > Order history
    • Find the purchase and tap "Request a refund"
  3. Contact Google Support:
    • Visit support.google.com/googleplay
    • Select "Contact us" for billing issues

Google Play's Refund Policy

  • 48-Hour Rule: Automatic refund eligibility for the first 48 hours
  • Extended Period: Case-by-case review for requests after 48 hours
  • Processing Time: Refunds typically process within 1-3 business days
  • Method: Refunds return to your original payment method
