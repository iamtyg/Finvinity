What's new in Finvinity - Version history and updates

🎉 Version 1.0.0 - Initial Release

Latest

Release Date: June 2025

🚀 Welcome to Finvinity!

We're excited to introduce Finvinity, a comprehensive, privacy-first portfolio tracking app that puts you in control of your financial data.

✨ Core Features

  • Multi-Asset Portfolio Tracking: Track stocks, cryptocurrencies, currencies, mutual funds, gold, and more all in one place
  • Real-Time Market Data: Live prices from Yahoo Finance, Alpha Vantage, and Finnhub APIs
  • Privacy-First Design: All data stored locally on your device - no cloud sync, no account required
  • Cross-Platform Support: Available on both iOS and Android with identical features
  • Portfolio Analytics: Comprehensive performance tracking with gains/losses calculations
  • Financial News Integration: Curated news relevant to your holdings from trusted sources

📱 Platform Support

  • iOS: Requires iOS 13.0 or later, optimized for iPhone and iPad
  • Android: Requires Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • App Size: Approximately 25MB download

🔒 Privacy & Security

  • No personal information required - use the app completely anonymously
  • All portfolio data stored locally using encrypted storage
  • No data collection beyond anonymous usage analytics
  • Full compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

📊 Supported Assets

  • Stocks: Major exchanges including NYSE, NASDAQ, international markets
  • Cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and 1000+ other digital currencies
  • Currencies: Major currency pairs and exchange rates
  • Mutual Funds: Wide selection of mutual fund offerings
  • ETFs: Exchange-traded funds from major providers
  • Precious Metals: Gold, silver, and other commodity tracking

💡 Key Highlights

  • 100% free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions
  • Works offline for viewing your portfolio (internet required for price updates)
  • Export and import capabilities for data portability
  • Clean, intuitive interface designed for all experience levels
  • Regular automatic price updates during market hours
  • Detailed portfolio performance analytics and charts

🧪 Beta Testing Program

Interested in trying new features before they're released? Join our beta testing program to get early access to upcoming updates and help shape the future of Finvinity.

How to Join

  1. Email us at finvinityapp@gmail.com with "Beta Testing" in the subject
  2. Include your device model and operating system version
  3. Tell us a bit about your investment experience and what features interest you most
  4. We'll send you instructions for accessing beta builds through TestFlight (iOS) or Google Play Console (Android)

What to Expect

  • Early access to new features before public release
  • Direct communication with the development team
  • Opportunity to influence feature development and design
  • Occasional surveys and feedback requests
  • Recognition in app credits (if desired)

⚠️ Beta Testing Disclaimer: Beta versions may contain bugs and incomplete features. We recommend backing up your portfolio data before installing beta versions.

🛠️ Support & Updates

We're committed to providing excellent support and regular updates to ensure Finvinity continues to meet your needs.

Update Policy

  • Regular Updates: New versions released every 2-3 months
  • Bug Fixes: Critical issues addressed within 1-2 weeks
  • Security Updates: Immediate patches for any security concerns

Getting Updates
