About Finvinity

Empowering investors with privacy-first portfolio tracking

Our Mission

At Finvinity, we believe that everyone deserves access to powerful financial tools without compromising their privacy. Our mission is to democratize portfolio tracking by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly application that respects your personal data and empowers you to make informed investment decisions.

We're committed to building financial technology that puts users first - no hidden fees, no data mining, no complicated sign-ups. Just a clean, powerful tool that helps you track and understand your investments across multiple asset classes.

Our Story

Finvinity was born out of a simple frustration: the lack of a comprehensive, privacy-focused portfolio tracking app that could handle multiple asset classes without requiring users to sacrifice their personal data or pay subscription fees.
