Quick answers to common questions about Finvinity

How do I download and install Finvinity?
Finvinity is available on both iOS and Android:
  • iOS: Search for "Finvinity" on the App Store or visit the App Store link on our website
  • Android: Search for "Finvinity" on Google Play Store or use our direct link
The app is free to download and requires no subscription or in-app purchases.
Do I need to create an account to use Finvinity?
No! Finvinity works completely without any account registration. Simply download the app and start tracking your portfolio immediately. This privacy-first approach means you don't need to provide any personal information like email, name, or phone number.
What devices are compatible with Finvinity?
iOS Requirements:
  • iOS 13.0 or later
  • iPhone 6s or newer
  • All iPad models from 6th generation onwards
Android Requirements:
  • Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • 2GB RAM minimum (4GB recommended)
  • 50MB available storage space

üì± Using the App

How do I add my investments to the portfolio?
Adding investments is simple:
  1. Tap the "+" button on the main screen
  2. Search for your asset using the ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL for Apple) or company name
  3. Select the correct asset from the search results
  4. Enter your purchase details: quantity, price paid, and date
  5. Add optional notes if needed
  6. Save your entry
The app will automatically track the current value and calculate your gains/losses.
What types of investments can I track?
Finvinity supports tracking multiple asset classes:
  • Stocks: Individual stocks from major exchanges (NYSE, NASDAQ, etc.)
  • Cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and hundreds of other digital currencies
  • Currencies: Foreign exchange rates (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc.)
  • Mutual Funds: Various mutual fund offerings
  • Gold: Precious metals tracking
  • ETFs: Exchange-traded funds
Can I edit or delete investments after adding them?
Yes! You have full control over your portfolio entries:
  • Edit: Tap on any investment to modify quantity, purchase price, date, or notes
  • Delete: Swipe left on an investment or use the delete option in the edit screen
  • Add Multiple Entries: You can add the same stock multiple times with different purchase dates and prices
All changes are saved automatically and immediately reflected in your portfolio calculations.

üîí Data and Privacy

Where is my portfolio data stored?
All your portfolio data is stored locally on your device only. We don't upload, sync, or store your personal investment data on any cloud servers or external databases. This means:
  • Complete privacy - only you can access your data
  • No internet required to view your portfolio
  • Your data stays with you even if our servers go down
  • You're responsible for backing up your data
Can I backup or export my portfolio data?
Yes! Finvinity includes data export features:
  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data
  • Choose between CSV or JSON format
  • Save the file to your device or cloud storage
  • Import data back using Settings ‚Üí Import Data
We recommend creating regular backups since data is stored locally only.

  • Export your data using the backup feature

  • You can reinstall the app later and import your data back

üìà Market Data

How often do prices update?
Price updates depend on the asset type and market conditions:
  • Stocks: Real-time during market hours, static when markets are closed
  • Cryptocurrencies: Updated every few minutes (24/7)
  • Currencies: Updated multiple times per hour during trading hours
  • Mutual Funds: Usually once per day after market close
Pull down on the main screen to manually refresh all prices.
Why are some prices delayed?
Some market data may be delayed by 15-20 minutes due to:
  • Exchange requirements for real-time data licensing
  • Third-party API limitations
  • Network connectivity issues
For the most current prices, we recommend checking official exchange websites or your broker's platform for critical trading decisions.
What if I can't find a specific stock or asset?
If you can't find an asset, try:
  • Using the exact ticker symbol (e.g., "AAPL" not "Apple")
  • Checking different exchanges (some stocks trade on multiple exchanges)
  • Searching by the full company name
  • Verifying the asset is publicly traded
If you still can't find it, contact us at finvinityapp@gmail.com with the asset details, and we'll investigate adding it.

üîß Technical Issues

The app keeps crashing. What should I do?
Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Force close and restart the app
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store/Play Store
  4. Ensure sufficient storage (at least 100MB free space)
  5. Check internet connection
If crashes persist, please contact support with your device model and iOS/Android version.
Prices aren't updating. How do I fix this?
Common solutions for price update issues:
  • Check internet connection - prices require internet access
  • Pull down to refresh on the main portfolio screen
  • Verify market hours - stock prices don't update when markets are closed
  • Wait a few minutes - sometimes there's a slight delay
  • Restart the app if prices seem stuck
How much data does the app use?
Finvinity is designed to be data-efficient:
  • Typical usage: 1-5 MB per month
  • Initial setup: 10-20 MB for downloading asset databases
  • Price updates: Very minimal data usage (few KB per update)
  • News articles: Additional data depending on usage
The app works fine on cellular data and won't consume excessive bandwidth.
